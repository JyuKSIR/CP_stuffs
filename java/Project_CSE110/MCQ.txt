1. What is the atomic number of Hydrogen?
a) 1
b) 2
c) 8
d) 16
Correct Answer: 1

2. Which of the following elements is a noble gas?
a) Oxygen
b) Nitrogen
c) Neon
d) Chlorine
Correct Answer: Neon

3. What is the pH of pure water?
a) 0
b) 7
c) 14
d) 10
Correct Answer: 7

4. Which of the following is a strong acid?
a) Acetic acid
b) Sulfuric acid
c) Citric acid
d) Carbonic acid
Correct Answer: Sulfuric acid

5. What is the chemical formula for sodium chloride?
a) NaCl
b) Na2CO3
c) NaHCO3
d) NaOH
Correct Answer: NaCl

6. Which gas is produced when an acid reacts with a metal?
a) Oxygen
b) Hydrogen
c) Nitrogen
d) Carbon dioxide
Correct Answer: Hydrogen

7. What is the process of converting a liquid into a gas called?
a) Melting
b) Freezing
c) Evaporation
d) Condensation
Correct Answer: Evaporation

8. Which element has the highest electronegativity?
a) Fluorine
b) Oxygen
c) Nitrogen
d) Chlorine
Correct Answer: Fluorine

9. What is the primary component of natural gas?
a) Ethylene
b) Methane
c) Propylene
d) Butane
Correct Answer: Methane

10. What type of bond is formed between two nonmetals?
a) Ionic bond
b) Metallic bond
c) Covalent bond
d) Hydrogen bond
Correct Answer: Covalent bond
